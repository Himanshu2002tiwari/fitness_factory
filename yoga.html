Power Yoga

Power yoga is a vinyasa-based (where flow and breath are matched) contemporary yoga technique that focuses on movement, cardio output, and strengthening exercises to achieve various objectives ranging from toning and strengthening the body to weight-loss. It is a dynamic physical workout.

Yoga is an ancient Indian practice for meditation, which evolved since BCE. Yoga is a set of poses or postures of the body called "Asana". There is a prescribed method to set oneself on the ground, to make the posture with the legs, hands, head and the entire body. Asanas had been explored, invented and documented in books. Yoga asanas are performed slowly, held for a minute or more. The muscles are exercised in the right way. One should not perform yoga after having food ie., with full stomach. Breathing in and breathing out air (called pranayama) is also part of Yoga. Yoga is very useful to people who cannot play sports or do jogging or do other demanding exercises like aerobics to keep fit. Yoga increases the health of body, mind. Cardio Yoga is a type of yoga class that combines Yoga moves with cardiovascular exercises. The workout takes classic yoga moves and makes them a bit faster, with a more rapid flow between sequences. The non-stop movement gets your heart beating and your muscles burning.
